Leveraging technology to secure your future

At 8wallet we strive to deliver a fully secure and private wallet experience. Explore the methods we employ to safeguard your journey below.

Security Features

Multi-Factor Authentication

We employ various methods to keep your account secure from unauthorized intrusions, including email & phone number confirmations.

Withdrawal Whitelist

Define a list of your trusted wallet addresses to allow outgoing transactions only to these addresses.

Activity Notifications

Each time you login into the system, a notification letter with details regarding login activity will be sent to you via email.

Your Keys. Your Privacy.

All private keys are stored only on your device and available for export at any time. No one can access your wallet or your personal data. Simple as that.

Real-Time System Monitoring

Our system’s transactional activity is being monitored 24\7 in order to identify and flag suspicious activity and prevent unlawful financial activities.

TLS\SSL 1.3 Encryption

When browsing the 8wallet website, all your personal info is encrypted and protected via a Transport Layer Security protocol v 1.3.

Pentesting Team

We’ve created a dedicated team that tests our own software solutions to deliver you the most secure experience on the market.

Bug Bounty Program

In order to leverage the expertise of the broader security research community, we’ve established a Bug Bounty Program. Every user can submit details about bugs and vulnerabilities found within our system and get a reward for taking part in the system's development.
